Description

In this game, you play as an engineer in the mits of travel, when your ship is shot down by Space Pirates.
Now you must navigate your way through a Space Pirate infested planetoid to survive.
This game plays much like a metroidvania, you are given a world to explore where you gradually gain more abilities to travel the world.

Power ups and Abilities

Shooter Bit:

      Allows players to fire projectiles to damage enemies and break certain walls. (In Demo)

Wall Jump:

     Enables additional jumps off of walls. (In Demo)

Double Jump:

      Grants a second Jump in mid air. (In Demo)

Dash:

      Gives players a burst of speed going forward for a short period. (In Demo)
